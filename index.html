My story

I spent 3 years and lakhs of rupees trying to break into TPM.
And I kept failing.

I wasn't unqualified. I wasn't lazy. I bought every course I could find, got certifications I thought would open doors, networked relentlessly, and applied to company after company.

Every time I made it a few rounds, then silence. No explanation. No feedback. Just another rejection I couldn't learn from. I watched people with fewer years of experience walk into TPM roles while I was stuck in the same loop.

The problem wasn't my skills. It was that nobody could give me a straight answer about what actually moved the needle. Generic course content. Recycled frameworks. Coaches who'd read about TPM but never sat in the chair.

So I stopped paying for shortcuts and started building the map myself. I reverse-engineered every successful transition I could study. I figured out what interviewers at companies like Akamai, Samsung, and Dell actually looked for — the positioning, the framing, the gaps people silently screened out for. I built a system out of three years of expensive, painful trial and error.

It worked. And then people started asking me how. I helped one person. Then five. Then more. Every single person I took through this framework moved faster than I did — because they didn't have to repeat my mistakes.

The 45-minute session I'm offering you today is the conversation I wish someone had given me at the start. Everything that took me 3 years — distilled, structured, and pointed directly at your specific background.

FAQs

Why is this a paid session? Most coaching calls are free.
Free strategy calls are sales calls. This is not one. You are getting a working session: a readiness assessment, positioning analysis, and a personalised written career roadmap built for your background. That work has value. The ₹299 also ensures both sides come prepared — people who have paid show up, do the work, and leave with something useful.
What exactly is the "free career roadmap"?
A written plan built in the session and delivered to you after: your readiness assessment, gaps to close, target company analysis, preparation timeline, and 3–5 prioritised next steps in order. Specific to your background — not a template you fill in yourself.
What makes this worth more than a course or a book?
Courses tell you what TPM is. This session tells you what YOUR path looks like — given your specific experience, targets, and gaps. Candidates who prepare without structured guidance typically spend 12–18 months, apply to 15+ roles, and get 2–3 interviews. Clarity on what's actually blocking you — and what to do in order — is worth several months of misdirected effort.
Will there be a sales pitch for a coaching programme?
If by the end of the session there's a clear case for ongoing mentorship and you're interested, Prerna will mention it. If you're not a fit — or it's not the right time — she won't push it. The session is focused on your clarity and your plan.
What if I find out TPM isn't the right move?
That's a useful outcome too. Getting honest clarity before spending months preparing is valuable in itself. If the fit isn't there, Prerna will tell you directly — and you can make a better-informed decision about your next move.
How long is the session and what happens after?
45 minutes, one-on-one. After the call, you receive your written career roadmap and next steps summary. Delivered to you — not something you write yourself during the call.
Can I reschedule or get a refund?
Yes to rescheduling — use the link in your Calendly confirmation. For refunds: cancellations made more than 24 hours before the session are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before are not eligible, as preparation time has been invested.
